You are given a string and your task is to swap cases. In other words, convert all lowercase letters to uppercase letters and vice versa.

For Example:

Www.HackerRank.com ? wWW.hACKERrANK.COM
Pythonist 2 ? pYTHONIST 2


Solution:-

def swap_case(s):
    a = ""
    for let in s:
        if let.isupper() == True:
            a+=(let.lower())
        else:
            a+=(let.upper())
    return a
